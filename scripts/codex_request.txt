## Required design change

Update `DecisionAttribution` so canonical block classification comes from the decision contract whenever possible.

The target structure is:

```text
decision_contract builds:
  primary_block_layer
  primary_block_reason
  all_block_reasons

  all_block_reasons
```

Do not treat `execution_block_reason` substring matching as the main source of truth. It may remain as a compatibility fallback only.
