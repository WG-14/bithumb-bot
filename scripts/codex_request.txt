
Do not let `target_delta`, `lot_native`, `residual_close`, and `operator_flatten` each invent separate final quantity semantics. They can have different intent sources, but final order plans must pass through shared invariant checks.

Required execution-plan invariants:

```text

  strategy_position
  target_delta
  residual_inventory
  operator_closeout

pre-submit proof status is observable
```

If an invariant is not enforceable because Bithumb allows a different market-sell convention, encode that as a named policy branch with tests and reporting fields. Do not leave it implicit.
